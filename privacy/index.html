Information We Collect

üì∑ Camera Permission

  • Purpose: The app uses camera access solely for QR code scanning to join focus sessions
  • Usage: Camera data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted
  • Control: You can revoke camera permission in your device settings at any time

üîß Accessibility Service (Android Only)

  • Purpose: Monitor which apps you open to detect and block distracting apps during focus sessions
  • Data Collected:
    • Names of apps you attempt to open during sessions
    • Timestamps of app usage attempts
    • List of installed apps for blocking selection
  • Data Processing: All app usage monitoring is processed locally on your device
  • Data Sharing: Only session participation status (staying focused vs. using blocked apps) is shared with group members - no specific app names or personal usage data
  • Privacy Protection:
    • Your personal app usage data never leaves your device
    • Group members only see whether you're staying focused, not which specific apps you used
    • All app blocking happens locally without sending data to our servers
  • Control: You can disable accessibility service at any time in Android Settings > Accessibility > peerpressure
